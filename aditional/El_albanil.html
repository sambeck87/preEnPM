Kapanga is an Argentine musical group of quartet, rock and ska. Formed in 1995, in the city of Quilmes, Province of Buenos Aires. The band is known for its great charisma and party rhythm in their shows.

The band's music is a fusion of genres with a predominance of happy and danceable rhythms. As a result, their concerts often resemble "parties". The singer, Martín Fabio, says "for me the musical part with Kapanga is always a party; there are many, many shows".
