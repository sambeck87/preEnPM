"Entre dos tierras" is the first song released as a single from the 1990 album Senderos de traición (the eighth single to that date) by the Spanish rock band Héroes del Silencio.

It was the No. 1 track on the Senderos de traición album and released as a promotional single in November 1990, accompanied by a sepia-toned video showing the band performing the song and, in other shots, a couple in violent situations.
