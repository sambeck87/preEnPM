La danza del fuego is Mägo de Oz's fifth single and the third and last one from the album Finisterra

The violin and flute give life to a classic medieval dance with harmonies in third, while the accompaniment of distorted guitars, bass and drums give it the characteristic Heavy-Fölk touch that the band possesses. The guitar solo is performed by guitarist Carlitos

The single also included a comic book called Cuentos de Satania, with the band members as main characters, which narrated the story of "Finisterra".
